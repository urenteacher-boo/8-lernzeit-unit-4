Step 4: The Challenge

Tell Your Partner About a Risk

Tell your partner about a risk that you sometimes take or that you have taken in the past. Explain how you protect or protected yourself.

Examples:
"I ride my bike quite fast, but I wear a helmet and I watch carefully for cars."
"Last year I went rock climbing, but I was with a guide and we used a rope."
